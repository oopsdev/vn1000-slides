Phase 8 bring-up - raw evidence

Level 2 keystone validated in the QEMU guest on a self-built QEMU 6.2.0 with the VN1000 device compiled in (-device vnic). See lessons-learned L-06 and TODO Phase 8.

  • Date: 2026-06-12
  • Host QEMU: qemu-system-x86_64 6.2.0 (Debian 1:6.2+dfsg-2ubuntu6.30), self-built tree at ~/qemu with hw/net/vnic.c (+ vnic_regs.h) and the meson/Kconfig hook.
  • Launch: sudo env QEMU="$HOME/qemu/build/qemu-system-x86_64" VNIC_DEVICE=1 VMDIR="$HOME/vnic-vm" ./run-qemu.sh
  • Module: vnic-l2.ko (out-of-tree, unsigned -> expected taint).
