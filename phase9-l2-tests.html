The 9 failures were a netdev-rename race, not the datapath

01-smoke "present" (1) and 02-lifecycle "missing on load" (8) failed because systemd-udev renamed the PCI netdev out from under the test:

[   87.689267] vnic 0000:00:03.0 vnic0: VN1000 Level 2 loaded (ID=0x564e3130, version=0x00010000, cap=0x00000003)
[   87.694002] vnic 0000:00:03.0 enp0s3: renamed from vnic0

The "interface present" checks ran in the ~5 ms window between register_netdev (vnic0) and udev finishing the rename to enp0s3; everything that ran after the rename settled passed. Root cause and fix (pin the name with NET_NAME_USER, commit 36fcc54) are in L-07.
